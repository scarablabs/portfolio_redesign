Animation –

Creating digital motion.

Make a move

Created for Jason Arena's Animation course during Spring 2019, I worked to explore the many ways to create movement, through 3D, 2D, and cel animated platforms.

In an effort to create an abstraction of Carl Sagan's words on the creation of the universe, I utilized Cinema 4D, and rendered volumes in Arnold to create the strange, shifting faces of the void.

Inspired by the likes of Blade Runner and Alien, I endeavored to create a startup sequence that emulated these CRT based pictures of what future screens would move and look like.
